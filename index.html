Welcome to The Clone Wars
Setup Complete

While your game downloads, here are some tips and tricks to help you get started on SWGTCW.

Account Creation

Don't forget to create a Game Account. Then use your Game Account information to sign into the game.

Community

Galaxy Chat: You can chat with other players in-game no matter where you are. Galaxy chat is tied into our Discord #general channel.

Auction Chat: Check the "Auction" channel in game to see where players are buffing and selling their goods. Right click a chat tab and add or modify a chat tab to include the "Auction" channel.

SWG Options

Use the "SWG Options" button in the launcher to configure your game.

Graphics: Set your Game Resolution to match your monitor's resolution. It is also recommended to run the game in both "Windowed Mode" and "Borderless Window" mode.

Sound: Make sure "Disable Audio" is NOT selected.

Game: If your computer performs well, "Disabled Character System LoD Manager."

Advanced: Check "Disable World Preloading" and "Disabled File Caching."
